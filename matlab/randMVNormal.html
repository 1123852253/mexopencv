cv.randMVNormal

Generates sample from multivariate normal distribution

samples = cv.randMVNormal(mu, sigma, nsamples)

Input

  • mu mean, an average row vector 1xd.
  • sigma symmetric covariation matrix dxd.
  • nsamples returned samples count.

Output

  • samples returned samples array nsamples-by-d.

Generates samples from multivariate normal distribution, with mean mu and covariance sigma.
