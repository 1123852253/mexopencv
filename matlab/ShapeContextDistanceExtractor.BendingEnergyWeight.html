cv.ShapeContextDistanceExtractor/BendingEnergyWeight

The weight of the Bending Energy in the final distance value.

The bending energy definition depends on what transformation is being used to align the shapes. The final value of the shape distance is a user-defined linear combination of the shape context distance, an image appearance distance, and a bending energy. default 0.3
