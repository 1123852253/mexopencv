cv.MotionSaliencyBinWangApr2014/computeSaliency

Compute the saliency

saliencyMap = obj.computeSaliency(img)

Input

  • img The input image, 8-bit.

Output

  • saliencyMap The computed saliency map. Is a binarized map that, in accordance with the nature of the algorithm, highlights the moving objects or areas of change in the scene. The saliency map is given by a matrix (one for each frame of an hypothetical video stream).

Performs all the operations and calls all internal functions necessary for the accomplishment of the Fast Self-tuning Background Subtraction Algorithm algorithm.
